Who are we?

The Undergraduate Poker Club provides an environment for Carnegie Mellon students to develop and apply analytical skills and strategies to the game of poker.

Our Membership

We are a relatively new organization in our second semester of operation. Our events gather up to 100 participants comprised of competitive students across numerous disciplines, including Computer Science, Engineering, Math, Finance, and Statistics.

Sponsorship Goals

We are seeking corporate partners to help subsidize our operating costs and promote the growth of our organization. In addition, we hope to facilitate connections between our members and companies interested in students with the analytical mindsets developed through poker.
